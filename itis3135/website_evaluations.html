Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is a great example of clean, modern design that follows CRAP principles.

    Contrast: Excellent use of contrast with dark text on light backgrounds and vice versa.

    Repetition: Consistent design patterns throughout the site create visual harmony.

    Alignment: All elements are properly aligned creating a clean, organized look.

    Proximity: Related items are grouped together logically.

Arngren

Arngren's website is a classic example of poor design that violates CRAP principles.

    Contrast: Poor contrast with clashing colors and no visual hierarchy.

    Repetition: No consistent design patterns - everything looks random.

    Alignment: Elements are scattered with no clear alignment.

    Proximity: Unrelated items are placed next to each other confusing users.
