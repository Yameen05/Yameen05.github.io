Soccer - My Passion

Soccer is my favorite sport. I love playing casually with friends on weekends, watching La Liga (especially Real Madrid), and following the Champions League and World Cup.

My favorite team is Real Madrid (RMA). Their history, players, and style of play inspire me. I also enjoy analyzing tactics and player performances.

Real Madrid Santiago Bernabéu Stadium

Cars - My Ultimate Love

I absolutely love cars. From drag races to street races, I’m obsessed with the speed, power, and engineering behind them. My dream car is a BMW M4 Competition, and I’m working hard to make that dream a reality when I have a stable job.

I’m a huge fan of German cars like BMW and Porsche, but I also have a soft spot for Japanese cars. The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 stole my heart after watching Fast and Furious, and the R35 is a beast. The Toyota Supra MK4 is another favorite, especially when people push it to insane levels like 3000 HP!

Coding - My Creative Outlet

Coding is more than just a skill for me; it's a way to express creativity and solve problems. I love the vibe it gives me when I build something from scratch.

Whether it's web development, scripting, or learning new technologies, coding keeps me engaged and motivated.

Gym - Staying Active

I go to the gym at least 3 times a week to stay fit and healthy. It's a great way to relieve stress and improve my overall well-being.

My routine includes weightlifting, cardio, and stretching. I love the feeling of progress and pushing my limits.

A modern gym with weights and equipment.

Travel - Exploring the World

I love the idea of traveling and exploring new places. Even though I haven't traveled much yet, I dream of visiting cities, mountains, and beaches around the world.

Traveling is about experiencing new cultures, meeting people, and creating unforgettable memories.
