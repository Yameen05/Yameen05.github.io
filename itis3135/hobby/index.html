Welcome

This site is dedicated to my love for soccer, my favorite club Real Madrid, and the one and only Cristiano Ronaldo! Explore to know more about me, how I play, and why soccer means the world to me!

About Me

My name is Yameen Alsaaidah. Soccer became my life the moment I saw Cristiano Ronaldo score a jaw-dropping free kick for Real Madrid on TV. Since then, I’ve been obsessed. I’ve played almost every day, dreaming about the Bernabéu and feeling the passion that soccer brings to my life. Whether watching Real Madrid lift trophies or hitting the pitch with my friends, soccer has become part of who I am.

Why Soccer?

Soccer makes me feel alive, free, and unstoppable. It clears my mind, sharpens my body, and fills me with purpose. The speed, the strategy, the heart-pounding moments—soccer is more than fitness, it's therapy and adrenaline in one. It’s where I’ve learned resilience, teamwork, and discipline.

Best Team: Real Madrid

Real Madrid. The kings of Europe. 14-time UEFA Champions League winners, 35 La Liga titles, a symbol of greatness. Legends like Zidane, Raul, Casillas, and of course Cristiano Ronaldo have worn the iconic white jersey. No team matches their legacy, their winning mentality, or their magic under the Bernabéu lights. Hala Madrid y nada más!

The GOAT: Cristiano Ronaldo

From Lisbon to Manchester, to Madrid, to Turin, and now Al Nassr. Cristiano Ronaldo is football royalty. 5 Ballon d'Ors, 5 Champions Leagues, over 800 goals, and an example of iron discipline. The highest scorer in both Real Madrid and international history. His work ethic, mindset, and love for the game made him my idol and the greatest to ever grace the pitch.

How I Play

I love playing on the wing, where I can use my speed to exploit space. I'm fast, sharp, and I try to play like Vinicius Jr. — quick dribbles, cuts inside, and lots of flair. I thrive on one-on-ones and delivering assists or cutting in for the shot. The feeling of leaving a defender behind is like no other!
